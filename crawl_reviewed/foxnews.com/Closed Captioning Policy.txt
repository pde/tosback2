
<p>Fox News is pleased to offer closed captioning on many Fox News programs made available online and through mobile apps.
Programs with closed captioning will have a "CC" icon in the player controls.
Just tap to enable or disable.</p>
<p>Certification Statement</p>
<p>To report an issue or concern regarding closed captioning on foxnews.com, please contact us at closedcaptioning@foxnews.com.
Please detail your issue or concern by providing the following information:</p>
<ul>
<li>Name of the program</li>
<li>Type of issue (e.g., garbling, missing captions on a certain episode, captions cut off at certain times or on certain days)</li>
<li>Website or application where you viewed the program</li>
<li>Approximate time and date that the issue occurred</li>
<li>Your name and contact information including mailing address, email and phone number</li>
</ul>
