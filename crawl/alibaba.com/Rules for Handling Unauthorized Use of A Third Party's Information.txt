<p>Alibaba.com has been committed to acting in good faith and actively maintaining a good market order of the platform.
In order to curb unauthorized use of others’ information and enhance user experience, these Rules are hereby formulated in accordance with the Rules for Transactions on Alibaba.com.</p>
<p> </p>
<p>
<strong>Article 1.
Definition of Unauthorized Use of </strong>
<strong>A Third Party</strong>
<strong>’s Information</strong>
</p>
<p>Users of Alibaba.com (“Alibaba.com”) shall not use the services provided on the website to engage in any acts using a third party’s information without the authorization of information owner, which include but are not limited to: </p>
<p>1.
Unauthorized use of a third party’s name</p>
<p>2.
Unauthorized use of a third party’s contact information</p>
<p>3.
Unauthorized use of a third party’s certificate</p>
<p>4.
Unauthorized publishing of a third party’s office photo</p>
<p>5.
Others</p>
<p>The principle for handling complaint is that the complainant should be the information owner and no complaint from a third party will be accepted.</p>
<p> </p>
<p>
<strong>Article 2.     Rules for Punishment of Unauthorized Use of A Third Party’s Information</strong>
</p>
<p> </p>
<p> </p>
<p>
<strong>Unauthorized Use of A Third Party’s Information</strong>
</p>
<p>
<strong>Response to Complaint</strong>
</p>
<p>
<strong>Punishment</strong>
</p>
<p>The first complaint is verified and the complainee deletes the improper information during the response period</p>
<p>Serious warning without deducting points</p>
<p>The first complaint is verified and the complainee fails to respond within the required response period</p>
<p>Deducting 2 points and deleting the improper information that includes a third party’s information </p>
<p>The second or subsequent complaint is verified</p>
<p>Deducting 6 points and deleting the improper information that includes a third party’s information</p>
<p>Notes: 1.
The response period is three working days.
If there is no response within such period, it shall be deemed as the complaint has been verified.</p>
<p>      2.
There is a protection period of 5 calendar days for the complaints about unauthorized use of a third party’s information, i.e., if several complaints are verified in 5 calendar days, the points will be deducted only once.
Such protection period is subject to the date on which the complaint is settled.</p>
<p>      3.
Where any company uses a third party’s information without authorization, causing material adverse effects, and refuses to cooperate, Alibaba reserves the right to delete the information from the complainee’s account.
</p>
<p>Refer to Rules for Enforcement Action against Non-compliance on Alibaba.com  for the standards for handling the corresponding accounts (unless otherwise specified, the penalty points are cumulative on the whole site of Alibaba.com)</p>
