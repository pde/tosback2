<br> Office Depot, Inc.

The company operates under several banner brands including Office Depot, OfficeMax and Grand &amp.
Toy.
The companys portfolio of exclusive product brands include TUL, Foray, Brenton Studio, Ativa, WorkPro, Realspace and Highmark.<br>
<br> Office Depot, Inc.s charitable arm, The Office Depot Foundation, supports programs that enhance quality of life for children, strengthen communities, encourage local and international economic growth, and empower schools and non-profit organizations.
