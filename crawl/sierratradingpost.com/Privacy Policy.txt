<p>Notice to California Residents - Your California Privacy Rights</p>
<p>If you are a California resident, California law provides that you be given the opportunity to opt out of our sharing your personal information with third parties that would use such personal information for their own direct marketing.
Absent such an opt-out, we may share your information as described in the "Privacy Policy" above.
Accordingly, per California law, we provide a way for you to opt out if you do not want us to share your information in this manner.
</p>
<p>If you are a California resident, you may ask us to refrain from sharing your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.
