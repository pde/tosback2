</ul> Like - Click this link to Add this page to your bookmarks Share - Click this link to Share this page through email or social media Print - Click this link to Print this page Free File Fillable Forms is Now Closed The program operates every year from January through October 15 <p>
<b>After October 20, each year:</b>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Every account is erased from the server</li>
<li>It is not possible to access any account or return information</li>
<li>It is not possible to print a return from an account you had created</li>
</ul>
<p>
<b>You cannot access your prior tax return information (tax years 2014 and earlier).</b>
</p>
<p>Information regarding prior year tax returns using Free File Fillable Forms:</p>
<ul>
<li>If you were not able to successfully e-file your return but printed a copy, mail it in.
 </li>
<li>If you do not have a copy to mail, you will have to prepare another. </li>
<li>For more information, go to our Online Individuals page.</li>
<li>Need to get up-to-date refund information? Go to Where's My Refund
</li>
<li>Need a copy of your federal return? If the IRS has accepted your return, Get a Transcript </li>
<li>Do you want to pay your balance or make estimated tax payments? Access Direct Pay
</li>
</ul>
