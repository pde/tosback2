</p> Privacy Policy FAQ <p>
<strong>How do I subscribe to your newsletter?</strong>
<br>
Just type your name, email and company name in the text boxes on the left near the top of the site, and click subscribe.</p>
<p>
<strong>Why do you need my email address?</strong>
<br>
So we know where to send the newsletter.<br>
<strong>
<br>
Why do you need my name and company name?</strong>
<br>
To see which companies have people subscribing to our newsletter.</p>
<p>
<strong>Do you share or sell my personal information with anyone else?</strong>
<br>
We don’t sell your personal information or share it with any third parties.
We do send advertisements from third party companies in our newsletter, but this comes from us.</p>
<p>
<strong>What do you consider personal information?</strong>
<br>
Any piece of information that can be used to uniquely identify you, such as your name or email address.</p>
<p>
<strong>How can I unsubscribe from your email newsletter?</strong>
<br>
There’s an unsubscribe link at the bottom of every issue.
Just click that link and follow the instructions on the next page.</p>
<p>
<strong>What else do you keep track of?</strong>
<br>
We use cookies to anonymously track traffic through our site.
Your web browser also provides information such as your IP address, browser version and language.
All of this is anonymous.</p>
<p>
<strong>Do your advertisers track me?</strong>
<br>
We’re an ad-supported business.
Our advertising provider may also use cookies to track consumer information and provide ads that you might find useful.<br>
