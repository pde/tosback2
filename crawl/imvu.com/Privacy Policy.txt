<p>

<br> If you wish to Join our VIP program, add credits to your account, purchase rights to use a particular avatar name, or purchase other services, we request certain PII from you on our order form.

<p>We use this information for billing purposes and to fill your orders.

<br> When you sign up for the Site or App you may choose to opt-in to receiving IMVU email.</p>
<p>We may send you email from time to time.

You may remove your photo(s), videos and other content at anytime by going to your account, selecting your content, and deleting content.
If you are a minor between the ages of 13 and 18, you may contact us via our help center to remove certain content that you have submitted on the Site and App.</p>
