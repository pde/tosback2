Criminal Subpoena Policy <p>GoDaddy.com, LLC Criminal Subpoena Policy</p>
<p>Go Daddy's Privacy Policy prohibits the release of customer or account information without express permission from the customer, except when required by law, to conform to the edicts of the law, or to comply with legal process properly served on Go Daddy or one of its affiliates.</p>
<p>If you seek the identity or account information of a Go Daddy customer in connection with a criminal matter, and you are a member of the law enforcement community, you must fax, mail, or serve GoDaddy.com, LLC with a valid subpoena.</p>
<p>
<strong> Submission of Subpoenas </strong>
</p>
<p>Go Daddy is headquartered in Scottsdale, Arizona and all criminal subpoenas should be served at that location or mailed to:</p>
<p>Compliance Department <br> GoDaddy.com, LLC <br> 14455 N.
Hayden Road <br> Suite 219 <br> Scottsdale, Arizona <br> 85260</p>
<p>Alternatively, the criminal subpoena can be faxed to:</p>
<p>(480) 624-2546 <br> Attn: Compliance Department</p>
<p>
<strong> Policies Regarding Email </strong>
</p>
<p>Go Daddy will not produce the content of e-mail, as the Electronic Communications Privacy Act, 18 U.S.C.
§2701 et seq., prohibits an electronic communications service provider from producing the contents of electronic communications, even pursuant to subpoena or court order, except in limited circumstances.
Go Daddy's e-mail servers do not retain deleted or sent e-mail.</p>
<p>Go Daddy reserves the right to request a copy of the complaint and any supporting documentation that demonstrates how the Go Daddy e-mail address is related to the pending investigation and the underlying subpoena.</p>
