<strong>Cookies</strong>
</p>
<p>This site, like many others, uses small files called cookies to help customize your experience</p>
<p>'Cookies' are small text files that are stored by the browser (e.g.
Internet Explorer or Safari) on your computer or mobile phone.
They allow websites to store such things as user preferences.
You can think of cookies as providing a "memory" for the website, enabling it to recognize a user and respond appropriately.</p>
<p>
<strong>Anonymous analytics cookies:</strong>
</p>
<p>Every time a user visits our website, web analytics software provided by a Google generates an anonymous analytics cookie.<br>These cookies can tell us whether or not you have visited the site before.<br>Your browser will tell us if you have these cookies, and if you don't, we generate new ones.
<br>This allows us to track how many individual unique users we have, and how often they visit the site.</p>
