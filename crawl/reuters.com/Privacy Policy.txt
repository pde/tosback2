Insight Online Privacy Statement SCOPE <p>Your privacy is important to Thomson Reuters and we go to great lengths to protect it.
This Privacy Statement applies to the online collection and sharing of personal and other information via the downloadable News &amp.
Insight desktop application and News &amp.
Insight mobile application operated by Thomson Reuters and its worldwide affiliated companies and subsidiaries.
This Statement does not apply to information collected in any other way, including offline, through Thomson Reuters websites, or through other Thomson Reuters mobile applications.
News &amp.
Insight may contain buttons or links to sites maintained by third parties.
These third party sites are not operated or controlled by Thomson Reuters and may have different privacy practices.</p>
<br>DEFINITIONS In this Privacy Statement: <ul>
<li>the terms “Personal Information” or “Personally Identifiable Information” means your name, address, phone number, email address, payment card information, and/or certain additional categories of information that identify you personally.
and</li>
<li>the terms “Personal Information” and "Personally Identifiable Information” do not include username, technical information (for example, Unique Device Identifier or “UDID,” Media Access Control “MAC” address, Apple’s Identifier For Advertising or “IFA,” and Internet Protocol or “IP” address), or numbers or alpha-numeric identifiers assigned by us, third-parties, or your computer.</li>
</ul>
<br>HIGHLIGHTS WHAT INFORMATION IS COLLECTED AND HOW IS THAT INFORMATION USED? <ul>
<li>We collect information about our users in the following ways: directly from the user (for example, when ordering a product, registering an account, or signing up for a newsletter), from our web server &amp.
application logs, through cookies, and through the use of local storage, including but not limited to local shared objects or “flash cookies,” as well as other files or similar technologies.
The device or devices on which you use News &amp.
Insight will determine, in part, the exact manner in which we collect information from you.</li>
<li>We also allow our analytics provider or providers to collect usage information over the Internet from your device, including a unique identifier that Thomson Reuters or the analytics provider assigns to your device, certain device and technical information, and your Internet protocol address.</li>
<li>We use the information, including analytics information, primarily to provide you with a personalized Internet or mobile application experience that delivers the information, resources, and services that are most relevant and helpful to you and to ensure quality of service.</li>
<li>We do not share your Personal Information, unless we say so in this Privacy Statement, or when we believe in good faith that the law requires it.</li>
</ul>

<li>Despite these measures, we do not guarantee that unauthorized persons will always be unable to defeat our security measures.</li>

<li>We rely on some of our affiliates for support of the products and services we offer, and we share some of our back-office functions with other Thomson Reuters companies.
our affiliates are all required to preserve the confidentiality of any Personal Information they may access.</li>
<li>We will not disclose any Personal Information except as necessary to service the account, to enforce the terms of use, to meet our obligations to content and technology providers, or as required by law.</li>

<br>Web Server Logs <p>When you use either the News &amp.
Insight desktop application or the News &amp.
Insight mobile application, we will track information about your use of the application and store that information in web server logs.
Our servers automatically capture and save the information electronically.
Examples of the information we may collect include:</p>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Your Internet protocol address</li>
<li>A unique identifier we assign to your device (or a unique identifier already associated with your device)</li>
<li>UDID, MAC address, IFA, or similar identifiers</li>
<li>Information or text that you enter in the application</li>
<li>The name of your Internet service provider</li>
<li>Other technical facts regarding your network access</li>
<li>The city, state, and country where you are using the application</li>
<li>The kind of operating system and device you use, including version number</li>
<li>Other technical facts regarding your device</li>
<li>Language preferences</li>
<li>The number of links or buttons you click within the application</li>
<li>The date and time of your visit</li>
<li>The content you viewed, purchased, or stored</li>
<li>Certain searches/queries that you conducted via the application</li>
</ul>
<br>
<p>The information we collect in web server logs helps us administer the applications, analyze their usage, protect the application and its content from inappropriate use, and improve the user's experience.</p>
<br>Local Storage (cookies and other technologies) <p>News &amp.
Insight desktop application.
If you use the News &amp.
Insight desktop application, we will use “local shared objects,” including a “flash cookie,” to store a unique identifier that we assign to you device.
The unique identifier consists of a number or alpha-numeric string and may be unique to your device, but does NOT contain Personal Information.
Our analytics provider has access to this unique identifier.
Additionally, we store your username as well as information regarding purchased content and use of the application in other local files on your computer.
This information is stored in order to help the application work.
The “flash cookies” and other files used by the News &amp.
Insight application can be deleted by uninstalling and deleting the application, but cannot be removed by deleting browser cookies, web cache, website data, temporary Internet files, or by using other browser controls or add-ons.</p>
<p>News &amp.
Insight mobile application.
If you use News &amp.
Insight on a mobile device, we will also store information locally on the device regarding the content that you purchase, access, and view, as well as your username.
This information is stored in order to help the application work.
This information is deleted from your device when you delete the application.
However, unique identifiers that are persistently associated with your device, including UDID, MAC address, or unique identifiers derived from your device’s technical information, will remain the same after deletion of the News &amp.
Insight application,</p>

These services may collect information sent by your device when you use the application, including IP address, type of device, operating system version, unique or persistent identifiers associated with your device (such as UDID, MAC address, IFA, or identifiers derived from your device’s technical information), network information, technical characteristics of your device or its software, language settings, and usage information relating to your use of the application.
If and when these third party services collect information, they do so without collecting Personal Information.</p>
