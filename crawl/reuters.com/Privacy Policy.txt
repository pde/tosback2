</ol> Comprehensive information and expert analysis <p>Now there's a single access point you can rely on for comprehensive information and expert analysis to help you stay informed and up to date on what's affecting your practice area.</p>
<ul>
<li>A dedicated team of attorney-editors monitors each practice area to ensure you see the most important updates of the day</li>
<li>Get exclusive practice area-focused content and expert analysis, including Westlaw Daily Briefings, Reuters Legal, Wolters Kluwer, CQ Roll Call, and more</li>
<li>Seamlessly access your Alerts on Westlaw to inform you promptly of new developments relevant to your practice area or matching your customized search</li>
<li>Select a Practitioner Insights page to be your Westlaw Start Page so you can start each day "in the know" </li>
</ul>
<p>
</p> See how<b> Practitioner Insights</b> work <ol>
<li>
<b>Westsearch:</b>  Appears at the top of every page and lets you do a global search of all content, or search within the practice area.<br>
</li>
<li>
<b>Top news</b>: A rotating display of the day's leading legal news stories from Reuters.<br>
</li>
<li>
<b>Make this my start page:</b> Just one click turns this page into your Westlaw Start Page.<br>
</li>
<li>
<b>Browse:</b> Market-leading content focused on your practice area, including the most comprehensive collection or primary law, exclusive analytical material.<br>
</li>
<li>
<b>Analysis:</b> This section includes analysis of the day's leading developments in your practice area from Wolters Kluwer Daily Reporting Suite, CQ Roll Call Washington Briefings, Westlaw Daily Briefings, and more.<br>
</li>
<li>
<b>My Alerts:</b> Create and manage all of your alerts on Westlaw.
A real-time feed of the most recent alerts is displayed.<br>
</li>
<li>
<b>Most popular analysis</b>: Front-and-center access to the top-viewed news and analysis.<br>
</li>
<li>
<b>Featured legal filings:</b> Selected by expert attorney-editors and deliver the most relevant, recently filed documents.</li>
</ol>
