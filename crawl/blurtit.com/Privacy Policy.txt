<p>
Blurtit uses standard methods to record data about your visit, storing 'cookies' on your computer to keep track of your preferences and activity on Blurtit.
These cookies are essential to the operation of Blurtit, and therefore we don't offer a facility to disable them.

<p>Blurtit is a real-name service.
If you sign up, your real name will displayed on your contributions to Blurtit for everyone to read.</p>
<p>We do not collect or store any personal information about you, unless you specifically supply details as part of our sign-up/register service or answer-reply service.</p>
<p>During your visit to Blurtit, we will collect certain pieces of information for reference purposes.
These will include only: </p>

<li>the time and date of your visit;</li>

Google;</li>

<p>All information gathered is not used to identify you personally, but only to collect data and statistics for analytical purposes.</p>
<p>Blurtit will use data to help improve the effectiveness of the website and user experience, by applying changes and enhancing the quality of page content.</p> We look after your information, and strive to correct any mistakes.
<p>
All personal information collected by Blurtit is held in accordance with the Data Protection Act which aims to protect user details and private information, and to handle data responsibly.
