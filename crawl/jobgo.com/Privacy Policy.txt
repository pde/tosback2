What information does JobGo collect from me? Depending on type of services used by the User JobGo may collect personally identifiable information, including but not limited to your name, company name, street address, e-mail address, telephone number, CV information and other recruitment based information as well as user and log info on Social Media use.

How does JobGo use the information collected from me? JobGo may use the information collected from you for the following purposes:

-	Fulfill any regulatory obligations Any kind of information received from you is not used or shared for non-JobGo related purposes.
