<br>Privacy and Security <ul>
<li> Overstock.com, Inc.
(“Overstock.com,” “Overstock,” “we,” “us,” or “our” as appropriate) has created the following Privacy and Security Policy to let you know what information we collect, why we collect it, and to whom it may be disclosed.
</li>
<li> This Privacy and Security Policy applies to http://www.overstock.com, http://www.o.info, and http://www.o.biz (the “Sites”).
</li>
<li> This Privacy and Security Policy also addresses our data security practices and the options you have to access and control personally identifiable and non-personally identifiable information.
</li>
<li> Our Terms and Conditions govern this Privacy and Security Policy.
</li>
<li> To the extent there is a conflict between the terms of this Privacy and Security Policy and the Terms and Conditions, the Terms and Conditions shall govern.
</li>
<li> We may periodically make changes to this Privacy and Security Policy.
</li>
<li> It is your responsibility to review this Privacy and Security Policy frequently and remain informed about any changes to it, so we encourage you to visit this page often.
</li>
<li> This Privacy and Security Policy supersedes any and all previous versions.
</li>
</ul>
<p>
<strong> Entering the Sites constitutes your acceptance and agreement to all terms contained in this Privacy and Security Policy.
If you do not agree with the terms set forth in this Privacy and Security Policy, please do not enter the Sites.
</strong>
</p>
<p>
<strong> INFORMATION WE COLLECT </strong>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
<strong> Personally Identifiable Information </strong>
<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li> The term “personally identifiable information” means information that you voluntarily provide to us and which personally identifies you, such as your name, phone number, physical address, and any other data or information that would allow someone to personally identify you.
</li>
<li> To make a purchase, we require you to supply us with personally identifiable information.
If you supply us with personally identifiable information, you may later access, update, and modify any inaccuracies.
</li>
<li> Please note, we generally keep a copy of the previous personally identifiable information for our records.
If you would like to review or change your personally identifiable information, you may do so at any time by accessing your account through the My Account link located at the top of each page.
</li>
<li> You may also choose not to provide personally identifiable information at the point of collection.
however, you will not be able to purchase products.
</li>
<li> In some areas of the Sites, you may have the ability to post or publish personally identifiable information.
You should take care and avoid posting or publishing personally identifiable information.
</li>
</ul>
<br>
<br>
</li>
<li>
<strong> Non-Personally Identifiable Information </strong>
<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li> The term “non-personally identifiable information” means information that you voluntarily provide to us or which we collect electronically, but which does not personally identify you.
</li>
<li> To register for an account, we require you to supply us with your email address.
Your email address does not personally identify you, but is a means for us to contact you.
You may later access, update and modify any inaccuracies by accessing your account through the My Account link located at the top of each page.
</li>
<li> Please note, we generally keep a copy of your previous email address(es) for our records.
</li>
<li> You may also choose not to provide us with your email address.
however, you will not be able to create an account or purchase products.
</li>
<li> Whenever you visit the Sites, non-personally identifiable information, such as navigational and clickstream data, is automatically collected.
</li>
<li> As you browse, we may install cookies (tiny text files stored on your computer, which record your preferences) in your browser.
Cookies cannot harm your computer and they do not contain any personally identifiable information.
You must accept cookies to shop through the Sites.
</li>
<li> You can erase or block cookies from your computer if you want to (your help screen or manual should tell you how to do this), but the Sites may not work correctly or at all if you set your browser not to accept cookies.
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p>
<strong> WHY WE COLLECT INFORMATION </strong>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
<strong> Personally Identifiable Information </strong>
<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li> We collect personally identifiable information in order to supply you with the products or services you request, process payment, perform internal analytics, communicate with you and comply with any legal requirements.
<br>
<br>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<strong> Non-Personally Identifiable Information </strong>
<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li> We collect non-personally identifiable information in order to operate, develop and improve the Sites, their functionality and our products, services and performance.
</li>
<li> We collect non-personally identifiable information in order to provide you with personalized recommendations for products advertised on or sold through the Sites.
</li>
<li> Non-personally identifiable information is collected in the aggregate to, among other things, determine the total number of users of the Sites, prepare utilization reports, diagnose and correct compatibility problems or other problems with the Sites and perform statistical analyses to enable us to build a higher quality, more useful online service.
</li>
<li> We also collect non-personally identifiable information to perform statistical analyses of user behavior and characteristics in order to measure interest in and use of the various aspects of the Sites.
By supplementing our records, this information helps us learn things like user preferences, popular search categories, click-through rates, what kinds of offers users like to see and in general, how to improve the functionality and relevancy of the Sites.
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p>
<strong> DISCLOSURE OF INFORMATION </strong>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
<strong> Personally Identifiable Information </strong>
<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li> We do not sell or rent personally identifiable information to third parties.
</li>
<li> We may disclose personally identifiable information to our staff and to third parties involved in the completion of your transaction, the delivery of your order, and analytics to support your use of the Sites.
</li>
<li> We may disclose personally identifiable information to trusted third parties whom we engage to process your personally identifiable information for us.
</li>
<li> We may disclose personally identifiable information if necessary to protect our legal rights, if the information relates to actual or threatened harmful conduct, or we have good faith belief that such action is necessary to (1) conform to the requirements of law or comply with governmental orders, court orders, or legal process served on us or (2) to protect and defend our property or other rights, the users of the Sites or the public.
</li>
<li> We may disclose personally identifiable information with other companies and organizations for fraud and credit risk purposes.
</li>
<li> We may disclose personally identifiable information to a financial institution and others for the purpose of jointly offering an Overstock.com credit card or Overstock.com credit offer.
</li>
<li> We will use commercially reasonable efforts to make you aware of the disclosure of your personally identifiable information contrary to the terms set forth in this Privacy and Security Policy – if, for example, we discover a security breach.
</li>
</ul>
<br>
<br>
</li>
<li>
<strong> Non-Personally Identifiable Information </strong>
<br>
<br>
<ul>
<li> We do not sell or rent non-personally identifiable information to third parties.
</li>
<li> We may disclose non-personally identifiable information to our staff and to third parties involved in the completion of your transaction, the delivery of your order, and the analysis and support of your use of the Sites.
</li>
<li> We may disclose non-personally identifiable information to trusted third parties whom we have engaged to process non-personally identifiable information for us.
</li>
<li> We may disclose non-personally identifiable information, if necessary, to protect our legal rights, if the information relates to actual or threatened harmful conduct, or we have good faith belief that such action is necessary to (1) conform to the requirements of law or comply with governmental orders, court orders, or legal process served on us, or (2) to protect and defend our property or other rights, the users of the Sites, or the public.
</li>
<li> We may disclose non-personally identifiable information to our affiliates and third parties in order to provide you with personalized recommendations for products advertised on or sold through the Sites.
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p>
<strong> THIRD PARTY ADVERTISING </strong>
</p>
<ul>
<li> We may engage third party advertising companies to advertise products or services to you online.
These companies may employ cookies and action tags (also known as single pixel gifs or web beacons) to measure advertising effectiveness.
They may also use this technology to serve specialized or relevant advertising content and/or recommendations to you on the Sites or on a third party’s website.
</li>
<li> Any information that these third parties collect via cookies and action tags is completely anonymous and does not contain any personally identifiable information.
</li>
<li> If you would like more information about this practice and your choices, including how to opt-out click here.
</li>
<li> Also, if you would like to opt out of Overstock using cookies for interest-based advertising purposes, please click here.
</li>
</ul>
<p>
<strong> PROTECTION OF INFORMATION WE COLLECT </strong>
</p>
<ul>
<li> We offer secure web pages to collect certain kinds of user information and we store certain kinds of data in encrypted form.
</li>
<li> We follow reasonable technical and management practices to help protect the confidentiality, security and integrity of data stored on our system.
</li>
<li> While no computer system is completely secure, we believe the measures implemented by our Sites reduce the likelihood of security problems to a level appropriate to the type of data involved.
</li>
<li> Our servers use Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) and encryption technology that works with Netscape Navigator, Microsoft Internet Explorer, Firefox, Safari, and AOL's browser, with the objective that only we can read a customer's personally identifiable information.
</li>
<li> We employ physical, electronic and procedural safeguards in connection with the collection, storage and disclosure of any personally identifiable information.
</li>
</ul>
<p>
<strong> LENGTH OF TIME WE KEEP INFORMATION </strong>
</p>
<ul>
<li> We generally keep user data on our server or in our archives for as long as we reasonably need it (for instance, for taxation or insurance purposes, or in case orders may give rise to claims).
</li>
<li> We may alter this practice according to changing requirements.
For example, we may delete some data, if needed, to free up storage space.
We may keep other data for longer periods if the law requires it.
</li>
<li> In addition, information posted in a public forum could stay in the public domain indefinitely.
</li>
</ul>

<li> We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites even if you access those using links from our Sites.
</li>
<li> Your use of these third party websites is entirely at your own risk and we recommend that you check the privacy and security policy of each website you visit.
</li>
<li> Clicking on a third party link will essentially take you to a third party’s website.
</li>
<li> We make no representation or warranty as to the effectiveness, quality, legitimacy or data protections of any third party website.
</li>
</ul>

<strong> SPECIAL TERMS FOR OUR NON-U.S.
CUSTOMERS </strong>
</p>
<ul>
<li> In order to process your order, you must provide your personally identifiable information directly to E4X, Inc.
(“E4X”) who also does business under the name “FiftyOne.” </li>
<li> E4X is one of our many fulfillment providers.
</li>
<li> E4X assists us in getting products to our international customers.
</li>
<li> E4X may also give you the opportunity to receive marketing messages from them, in which case you should refer to their terms and conditions for details about how they will use your data for that purpose.
</li>
<li> E4X has assured us that they will process your personally identifiable information with at least the same level of privacy protection as set forth in the US-EU Safe Harbor Framework and US-Swiss Safe Harbor Framework as set forth by the US Department of Commerce regarding the collection, use, and retention of personal information from European Union member countries and Switzerland.
Clicking on the checkout button on the Sites redirects you to a checkout page hosted by E4X to complete your order.
</li>
<li> On the checkout page, you are required to select a method of payment.
</li>
<li> You will be required to submit credit card and personally identifiable information to E4X to complete your order.
</li>
<li> Upon completion and approval of your order by E4X, E4X will notify us of the approval or denial of your order.
</li>
<li> Once notified by E4X of your approval, we will process your order and ship the product directly from our warehouse.
or cause one of our third party fulfillment providers to ship the product directly from their warehouse, to E4X.
</li>
<li> E4X will thereupon purchase the product from us thereby taking title to the product(s), bill your credit card, collect and remit any duties and taxes to the appropriate taxing authority and arrange for delivery of the product to your doorstep by common carrier.
</li>
<li> In this process, E4X makes the sale to you as the merchant of record, but we are legally obliged to deliver the product(s) ordered as set out in our Terms and Conditions.
</li>
<li> If you have any questions about products ordered, you should direct them to us and not to E4X.
</li>
<li> Ordering a product will present you with E4X’s terms and conditions to which you must agree in order to receive the product(s) you ordered.
</li>
<li>
<strong> If there is any inconsistency between this Privacy and Security Policy and those of E4X, this Privacy and Security Policy will prevail.
</strong>
</li>
<li> Any personally identifiable information you provide us is controlled and processed by Overstock.com, Inc., 6350 South 3000 East, Salt Lake City, Utah 84121 or its suppliers (where indicated in this Policy).
As mentioned above, your personally identifiable information provided at checkout will be controlled and processed by E4X, Inc., 55 West 39th Street, 18th Floor, New York, New York 10018.
If you choose to provide us and/or E4X with personally identifiable information, you consent to the transfer and storage of that information on servers located in the United States.
</li>
<li> Overstock.com complies with the US-EU Safe Harbor Framework and US-Swiss Safe Harbor Framework as set forth by the US Department of Commerce regarding the collection, use, and retention of personal information from European Union member countries and Switzerland.
Overstock.com has certified that it adheres to the Safe Harbor Privacy Principles of notice, choice, onward transfer, security, data integrity, access and enforcement.
To learn more about the Safe Harbor program, and to view our certification page, please visit http://www.export.gov/safeharbor/.
</li>
<li> In compliance with the US-EU and US-Swiss Safe Harbor principles, Overstock.com commits to resolve complaints about your privacy and our collection or use of your personal information.
European Union or Swiss citizens with inquiries or complaints regarding this Privacy and Security Policy should first contact Overstock.com at: Overstock.com, Attn: Customer Security Department, 6350 South 3000 East, Salt Lake City, Utah 84121 </li>
