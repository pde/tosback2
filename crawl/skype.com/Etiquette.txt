 Etiquette <p>Skype is a community, and members of a community must respect each other as human beings.
Skype therefore asks you not to get involved in any unwanted profane, offensive, obscene or sexual exchanges or to attempt to harm a young person in any way.</p>
<p>Please go to Inhope’s site if you would like information on reporting illegal use and content on the internet.
</p>
<p>Skype asks that you do not:</p>
<ul>
<li>Use vulgar, profane, offensive or obscene language.</li>
<li>Post or request sexually explicit images or other offensive content.</li>
<li>Exploit a minor in any way.</li>
<li>Use racially, religiously or sexually offensive language.</li>
<li>Discuss illegal activity, such as how to get drugs and how to make bombs.</li>
<li>Ask for or offer sexually explicit images, and/or material harmful to children.</li>
<li>Harass, threaten, embarrass, or do anything else to another Skype user that is unwanted.</li>
<li>Infringe anyone else's intellectual property rights, including but not limited to any copyright, trademark, rights of publicity, or other proprietary rights.</li>
<li>Impersonate or represent Skype.</li>
<li>Link to content not allowed on Skype.com.</li>
<li>Try and get a Skype’s member's password or other account information.</li>
<li>Post or request personal or other information from a minor (any person under the age of 18).</li>
</ul>
